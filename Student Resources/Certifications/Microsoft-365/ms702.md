## ðŸ§ª Practice Questions

### Question 1: Teams Deployment
**Your organization currently uses Skype for Business. You want to allow users to use both Skype and Teams simultaneously during migration. Which coexistence mode should be used?**

A) Islands  
B) Skype only  
C) Teams only  
D) Teams and Skype  

**Answer: A) Islands**  
*Islands mode allows Skype for Business and Teams to operate independently side by side.*

### Question 2: Teams Governance
**You need to ensure all Teams in your organization follow a naming convention like "DEPT-ProjectName". What feature should you implement?**

A) Team templates  
B) Naming policies  
C) Team classification  
D) Retention policies  

**Answer: B) Naming policies**  
*Teams naming policies enforce consistent naming conventions across your organization.*

### Question 3: Meeting Security
**You want to prevent external attendees from joining Teams meetings without a specific lobby approval. Which policy should be configured?**

A) Meeting retention policy  
B) Lobby policy  
C) Recording policy  
D) Transcription policy  

**Answer: B) Lobby policy**  
*The lobby policy controls how external attendees join meetings and can require approval.*

### Question 4: Guest Access
**An external partner needs to participate in a Teams channel for 3 months. What is the recommended approach?**

A) Make them a permanent team member  
B) Add them as a guest and track access with a retention policy  
C) Create a separate Teams just for them  
D) Email meeting links for each meeting  

**Answer: B) Add them as a guest and track access with a retention policy**  
*Guest access with tracking is the proper governance approach for temporary external participation.*

### Question 5: Teams Usage Analytics
**You notice heavy Teams usage on weekends in your organization. Which tool should you use to investigate this pattern?**

A) Teams activity report  
B) Call analytics  
C) Device usage report  
D) Message analytics  

**Answer: A) Teams activity report**  
*Teams activity reports show usage patterns including days and times of peak activity.*
