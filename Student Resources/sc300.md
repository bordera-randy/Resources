## üéØ Tips & Study Strategy
1. **Hands-on practice** ‚Äî create Entra tenants, connect on-prem AD, and configure Conditional Access.  
2. **Understand all identity models** ‚Äî cloud-only, hybrid, B2B/B2C, and federation.  
3. **Know Conditional Access deeply** ‚Äî signals, policies, user/session risk, and MFA enforcement.  
4. **Review governance features** ‚Äî PIM, access reviews, entitlement management, lifecycle workflows.  
5. **Practice with external identities and app SSO integrations.**  
6. **Use KQL and auditing logs** for monitoring and reporting.
