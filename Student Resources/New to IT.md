## 🛠 How to Use This Guide
1. **Explore**: Browse categories that align with your learning goals.  
2. **Learn**: Use the free or affordable training platforms listed below.  
3. **Practice**: Try hands-on labs and sandbox environments.  
4. **Certify**: Validate your skills through vendor certifications.  
5. **Engage**: Join online communities and share your journey.  
6. **Contribute**: Found a great resource? Submit a pull request!  

📌 *This guide is updated regularly to include new trends and learning opportunities.*
